About Our Team

Esperto Labs is a brand-new design team from the University of Waterloo interested in creating wearable devices and giving students and young developers the opportunity to learn about software and electronic design. Our team is building the Esperto Watch which is a watch containing numerous sensors, wireless connectivity, and is open-source. In addition to a high-tech smart watch and an integrated web and mobile application, users are able to track and perform data analysis on raw sensor information obtained throughout the data using the watch including accelerometer and heart rate data. Our platform also gives users the ability to implement their own algorithms and hardware to make our platform specific to their intended application. This removes the tedious task of having to design and build a wearable from scratch to test a new sensor or obtain data to perform analysis on afterwards.
